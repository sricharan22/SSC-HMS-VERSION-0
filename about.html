SSC HOSPITALITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

SSC Cloud is a renowned software company with headquarters in Hyderabad. SSC produce and support Online Booking, Channel Management and Front Office Systems to the world’s hospitality industry.

SSC has been at the forefront of development of property and reservations management systems for over 5 years for the Holiday Park, Caravan and RV Park, Hotel, Motel and Apartment segments with specialty applications for the Mining, Defence, Facility and Shopping Centre industries.

The SSC Property Management System offers the unique ability to provide real-time online booking functionality, which enables the collection of bookings from a property’s website, the global distribution system and an expansive variety of third-party travel websites.

Currently, SSC host an expanse of over 6,000 properties across 43 cities utilizing the vital ability to complete online bookings, maintain channel management and operate a front office system in a single application.
